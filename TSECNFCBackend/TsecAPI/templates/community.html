The Global March Against Child Labor:

GMA is a global network that works to end child labor worldwide. While their primary focus is on child labor, they also address related issues such as human trafficking and forced labor that can affect adult men. GMA works with governments, NGOs, and communities to raise awareness, advocate for policy change, and provide direct support to victims.
