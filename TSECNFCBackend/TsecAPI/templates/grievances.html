Steps to Share Your Human Trafficking Experience

  1. Step 1: Gather information by reflecting on your experiences and collecting evidence.
  2. Step 2: Find support by reaching out to trusted individuals or human trafficking support organizations.
  3. Step 3: Decide how to share your story anonymously or publicly, and choose a platform.
  4. Step 4: Submit the form and keep a record of the submission reference.
  5. Step 5: Craft your narrative by focusing on your experiences, using your own voice, and considering the impact.
  6. Step 6: Share your story by publishing your narrative and connecting with others.

Who Will See Your Story

The stories will be reviewed by:

  • Human trafficking support organizations
  • Law enforcement agencies
  • Legal professionals and advocacy groups
